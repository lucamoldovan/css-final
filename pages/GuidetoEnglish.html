Beginner's Guide to English

Course Level: Beginner

Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Vocabulary

Match the English words with their Spanish translations:

  • House: Casa
  • Dog: Perro
  • Book: Libro
  • Apple: Manzana

Exercise 2: Conversation

Complete the following conversation in English:

Person A: Hello! How are ?

Person B: I'm fine, thank you. And you, how are ?

Exercise 3: Fill in the Blanks

Fill in the blanks to complete the sentences in English:

1. I really like to the movies on weekends.

2. Next year, I want to to Spain.

3. Where you? In Barcelona.

Exercise 6: Translate Questions

1. How old are you?

2. What is your favorite color?

3. When do you usually wake up?
